Chilli Buttery Pasta

It's literally pasta with butter on it and spicy

Ingredients

For the pasta

  • 1 bag of store bought pasta

For the butter

  • 1 tbsp of butter (any kind)

Toppings

  • Chilli Oil
  • Chilli Flakes

Step by Step:

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. When done boiling pour out water and put butter in
  3. Mix until melted
  4. Pour chilli oil and chilli flakes in
  5. Success
