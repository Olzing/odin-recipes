American Hot Pizza

Bloody delicious this is, absolutely delicious

Ingredients

For the base

  • 30g Flour
  • 1 tspSalt
  • 1 tbspFast acting Yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar

Toppings

  • Pizza sauce
  • Chilli Oil
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Jalapenos
  • Pepperoni

Step by Step:

  1. Mix the flour salt, yeast and sugar into a mixing bowl
  2. When all nice and normal start kneading it for 10 minutes
  3. Leave for 30 minutes for dough to rise
  4. Make the shape of a pizza base with hands and rolling pin
  5. Pour pizz sauce on
  6. Add whatever toppings you want
  7. slam it in a pizza oven for 120 seconds
  8. BOSH
